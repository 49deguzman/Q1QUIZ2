Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

Teenager Miles Morales suddenly gains powers from a spider and becomes his universe's new Spider-man. However, his universe is put under threat to a man named Kingpin, who seeks to activate a machine that creates a black hole in New York and possibly in the Multiverse. He pairs up with 5 alternate versions of himself to stop him.
